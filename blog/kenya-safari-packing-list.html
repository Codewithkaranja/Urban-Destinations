Kenya Safari Packing List (2026)

A practical checklist for comfort, safety, and better photos — built for Kenya’s early game drives and warm afternoons.

Updated: 2026 Read time: ~7 minutes Category: Packing

Safari packing mindset (pack light, pack smart)

Safari days are simple: early mornings, dusty roads, warm afternoons, and a relaxed evening. Your goal is to stay comfortable and prepared — not to bring your entire wardrobe.

Golden rule: Pack for layers (cold mornings + warm midday) and choose neutral colors that don’t attract attention or dust.

Clothing essentials

  • 2–3 neutral tops: breathable, long-sleeve option helps with sun + bugs.
  • 2 comfortable trousers/jeans: avoid very bright colors.
  • 1 warm layer: fleece or light jacket for early drives.
  • 1 rain layer: compact rain jacket (especially in shoulder seasons).
  • Sleepwear + light evening outfit: for lodge dinners.
  • Swimwear: many camps/lodges have pools.

Footwear

  • Comfortable closed shoes (sneakers or light hiking shoes).
  • Sandals/slides for lodge relaxation.
  • Optional: light hiking boots if you’re doing serious walking activities.

Health & safety kit

  • Personal medication + basic pain relief.
  • Insect repellent + soothing cream (after-bite).
  • Sunscreen + lip balm (sun + wind).
  • Hand sanitizer + wet wipes (very useful on the road).
  • Plasters/band-aids for blisters.

Tech & photo gear

  • Phone + charger + power bank.
  • Extra storage (memory card/phone space).
  • Camera (optional) + spare battery.
  • Binoculars (optional but highly recommended).

Documents & money

  • ID/Passport + copies (photo on phone is helpful).
  • Booking confirmation and contact numbers.
  • Cash for tips and small purchases (where needed).

Smart extras most people forget

  • Dust-friendly scarf/buff for windy drives.
  • Reusable water bottle.
  • Small daypack for game drives.
  • Sunglasses + hat/cap.
Simple printable checklist: Clothes (layers), closed shoes, repellent, sunscreen, power bank, water bottle, meds, ID, cash for tips.
