Zanzibar from Kenya: Planning Guide

Documents, costs, where to stay, and how to plan a premium Zanzibar trip that feels smooth from start to finish.

Documents & planning essentials

Cross-border trips feel premium when paperwork is handled early. Keep your documents organized and avoid last-minute stress.

  • Valid travel document + copies (digital copy helps).
  • Confirm your flight details and baggage allowances.
  • Book transfers early (airport → hotel).
Premium tip: Choose your hotel area first (quiet beach vs lively zone). It affects your entire trip vibe.

Where to stay (choose the experience)

Stone Town (culture)

Best for history, architecture, markets, and short cultural stays (1 night works well).

Beach zones (relaxation)

Best for resort-style calm, swimming, and island time. Great for 2–5 nights.

Costs: what to budget for

  • Flights (vary by season and booking timing)
  • Accommodation tier (comfort vs luxury)
  • Transfers and day excursions
  • Meals and curated dining

Suggested itinerary (4 days)

  • Day 1: Arrive + settle + sunset dinner
  • Day 2: Beach + optional water activity
  • Day 3: Excursion day (choose 1 main experience)
  • Day 4: Slow morning + checkout

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Trying to do too many excursions (you’ll feel rushed).
  • Not planning transfers (airport movement matters a lot).
  • Choosing a hotel location that doesn’t match your vibe.
